Code of Conduct

Purpose

Our community exists to foster collaboration, learning, and growth in a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free environment for everyone, regardless of background or skill level.

Expected Behaviour

We expect all members to:
Be respectful and considerate in speech and actions.
Embrace diversity of thought, background, and experience.
Assume good intent; ask questions before jumping to conclusions.
Use inclusive language and avoid offensive, derogatory, or dismissive remarks.
Give constructive feedback, not personal attacks.
Help create a welcoming, supportive space for newcomers.

Unacceptable Behaviour

The following will not be tolerated:
Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation of any kind
Hate speech, racist or sexist remarks, or slurs
Personal attacks, threats, or trolling
Demeaning jokes or language (including in usernames or avatars)
Disruption of talks, forums, events, or group activities
Unwanted sexual attention or advances

Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct:
Please report it to the community moderators, organisers, or maintainers. Reports can be made privately and confidentially. We will respond promptly and take appropriate action, including warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal.

Enforcement

Community leaders reserve the right to:
Remove posts, comments, or contributions that violate this code.
Warn or ban individuals who repeatedly or seriously breach expectations.
Protect the integrity of the community and ensure everyone feels safe.
